Hometown

Jakarta

Jakarta is the vibrant capital city of Indonesia, known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse population, and dynamic urban life. As the center of government, business, and entertainment, Jakarta blends modern skyscrapers with historical landmarks, traditional markets, and a variety of local cuisines. Despite its fast-paced lifestyle, the city holds strong ties to its Betawi roots, offering a unique mix of tradition and modernity.
